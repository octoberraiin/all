The African pygmy hedgehog is the species of miniature hedgehog most commonly kept as a pet.
When threatened, the little hedgehog will raise its spines to look larger. If the threat continues, the
hedgehog will tuck it's head and feet and roll up, leaving a prickly ball for confused predators to deal with.

For more information about African Pygmy hedgehogs, check out this website!

What to feed them?

  1. Specialised hedgehog food ( of course! )
  2. High-quality dry cat/dog food
  3. Boiled or scrambled eggs
  4. Lean meats
  5. Insects - it's their favorites
  6. Some fruit & veg
  7. Baby food
